Welcome to Café Marameo

A Taste of Italy in Berlin

Café Marameo is a charming Italian café nestled in the vibrant Kreuzberg neighborhood of Berlin. Run by an Italian brother and sister duo, we bring authentic Italian café culture to the heart of the city.

We serve exceptional coffee from Passenger Coffee, expertly prepared to highlight the unique characteristics of each blend. Our menu features homemade Italian pastries, savory treats, and carefully crafted beverages.

Whether you're looking for your morning cappuccino, a leisurely afternoon break, or a cozy spot to meet friends, Café Marameo offers a warm, welcoming atmosphere that feels like home.
